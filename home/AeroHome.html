    About us


    The Aeromodelling club works mainly on developing drone technology. We work on making drones autonomous and smart and also develop RC planes as a hobby.

    The Aim of Aeromodelling Club, IITG is to develop smart drones with an easy interface for common people to interact with drones. The motive behind this aim is that drone technology would revolutionize the tech industry in the next decade. Be it the transportation industry or agricultural industry, drone technology would have a huge impact on transforming tech industries. Aeromodelling Club aims to contribute to this transformation.
